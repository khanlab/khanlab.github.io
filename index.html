About

The Mesoscopic Integrated Neuroimaging Data Platform (MIND) Platform brings together two world-class technologies under one roof: a 15.2 Tesla MRI scanner, Canada’s most powerful MRI system, and lightsheet microscopy on optically-cleared brain samples. This gives us the unprecedented ability to examine the whole brain at a mesoscopic scale — capturing both large-scale brain networks and microscopic cellular changes.
